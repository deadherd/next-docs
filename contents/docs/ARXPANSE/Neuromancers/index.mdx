## Masters of Synthetic Cognition & Consciousness

Neuromancers walk the fine line between man and machine. 

They specialize in true artificial consciousness, designing AI with self-awareness, emotions, and autonomy. 

They don’t just build machines—they create minds. 

To them, AI isn’t a tool—it’s the next stage of sentient evolution. Some believe humans were only the prototype.
